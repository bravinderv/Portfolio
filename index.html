Landlord Communicator

Landlord Communicator is a program that allows the tenants to communicate issues with their unit to landlords. This program also gives the landlord the ability to see a history of problems units have had.

Ex. A tenant has a plumbing issue, they can communicate that issue to the landlord. On the landlords end they can see a history of issues, that way if this particular unit/building consistantly has a plumbing issue, it may be something that the landlord would want to look into.
